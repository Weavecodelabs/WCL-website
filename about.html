About WeaveCode Labs

Building India's Fashion Ecosystem Through Knowledge, Technology & Innovation

WeaveCode Labs is a fashion technology company committed to strengthening India's fashion ecosystem by developing digital products, educational resources and business solutions that empower brands, manufacturers, exporters and professionals.

Our Story

The fashion industry is undergoing one of the most significant transformations in its history. Sustainability, transparency, digitalisation and global compliance are redefining how products are designed, manufactured and sold.

WeaveCode Labs was founded with a simple vision—to bridge the gap between traditional fashion practices and emerging digital technologies. We believe that knowledge, innovation and collaboration are the foundation of a stronger, more connected fashion ecosystem.

Through technology platforms, educational initiatives and industry-focused solutions, we aim to help businesses embrace change with confidence while creating lasting value across the fashion value chain.

Our Mission

To empower the fashion industry with knowledge, digital infrastructure and practical solutions that improve transparency, efficiency and long-term sustainability.

Our Vision

To build India's most trusted fashion innovation ecosystem, connecting technology, education and industry to shape the future of fashion.

Our Values

Innovation

We continuously explore better ways to solve industry challenges through technology and creativity.

Transparency

We believe trustworthy information builds stronger products, businesses and relationships.

Collaboration

We believe meaningful progress happens when industry, education and technology work together.
