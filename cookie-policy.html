1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how WeaveCode Labs India Private Limited ("WeaveCode Labs", "we", "our" or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, remember user preferences and improve the overall browsing experience.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Improve website performance and functionality.
  • Remember user preferences where applicable.
  • Maintain website security.
  • Understand how visitors interact with our website.

4. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly and cannot be disabled.

We may use analytics services to understand website traffic, visitor behaviour and improve user experience. These cookies collect aggregated and anonymous information.

Functional Cookies

These cookies may remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience on future visits.

At present, WeaveCode Labs primarily uses essential cookies required for the operation of the website. Additional analytics or functional cookies may be introduced in the future, and this policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control or disable cookies through their settings. You may delete existing cookies or prevent new cookies from being stored. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the website.
